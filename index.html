About

Shreya Verma,
producer first,
director always.

A graduate of Lady Shri Ram College with Honours in Journalism, I started out making student films, learning to tell stories before I knew what a call sheet was.

That foundation led me into end-to-end production, where I discovered I was equally at home building a budget as I was behind the camera. Since then, I have worked with numerous brands across telecom, tech, banking, real estate, and consulting. Different rooms, same job: figure out what a brand actually needs to say, then say it without wasting a frame.

My work spans brand films, viral reels, and modern-style documentaries. I bring both the strategic and the creative to every brief.
